About soft computing and Knowledge Engineering:

What is soft computing and Knowledge Engineering:
Soft computing is an umbrella term that covers the various integral paradigms of intelligent systems such as genetic algorithm, neural networks, computational intelligence, evolutionary computing, fuzzy logic along with other tools of intelligent information processing. Soft computing emphasizes the synergism among these paradigms of intelligence in solving the many open and difficult world problems of humanity today.

Objective:

Target of this lab is to become an international scientific centre in soft computing and knowledge engineering by the following interests:
⦁ Tackle basic issues of information science by the tools of soft computing and knowledge engineering.
⦁ Advance the state of the art on the theory of soft computing and knowledge engineering.
⦁ Establish the Lab in intelligent systems and knowledge engineering nationally and internationally.
⦁ Introduce soft computing, knowledge engineering and intelligent information processing to the society and the industrial/academic experts.
⦁ Form national and international teams of academicians and industry experts to solve the problems through the application of intelligent systems and knowledge engineering.
⦁ Organize Invited Lectures, Seminars, Workshops and Conferences to generate knowledge in Soft Computing and Knowledge Engineering.
⦁ Collaborate with industries for higher efficiency, improved design and better decisions.
⦁ Develop systems for knowledge-based enterprises.
